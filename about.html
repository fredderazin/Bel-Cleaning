WHO WE ARE

Professional Cleaning You Can Count On

Bel Cleaning is a full-service commercial and residential cleaning company based in St. Louis, Missouri. We provide reliable, high-quality cleaning solutions for offices, warehouses, healthcare facilities, data centers, and homes throughout the greater St. Louis metro area.

Founded on the principles of hard work, integrity, and attention to detail, Bel Cleaning has built a reputation as a trusted partner for businesses and homeowners who demand consistent, professional results. Every member of our team is background-checked, insured, and trained in OSHA safety standards and industry best practices.

OUR MISSION

Delivering Clean Spaces, Building Trust

Our mission is to provide exceptional cleaning services that create healthier, more productive environments for our clients. We believe a clean space is the foundation of a successful business and a comfortable home.

Reliability

We show up on time, every time. Our clients trust us to deliver consistent results shift after shift, and we never cut corners.

Quality First

We use professional-grade equipment and proven cleaning methods. Every job is inspected to meet our high standards before we leave your facility.

Safety & Compliance

OSHA-trained staff, proper chemical handling, and eco-friendly product options. We prioritize the health and safety of your building occupants.

Clear Communication

Dedicated account managers, transparent pricing, and responsive support. We make it easy to work with us and address any concerns quickly.
