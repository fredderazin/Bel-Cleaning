Post-Construction Cleaning Done Right

Construction projects leave behind more than just a finished building. Drywall dust, paint overspray, adhesive residue, scuff marks, and debris can linger on every surface, making a brand-new space look unfinished and uninviting. Bel Cleaning's post-construction cleaning services transform your project from a construction zone into a move-in ready facility, no matter the scale.

Our trained crews understand the specific challenges of construction cleanup. We follow a phased approach — rough clean, final clean, and punch-list touch-ups — to ensure every surface, fixture, and corner meets your standards and the expectations of building owners, tenants, and inspectors.

What's Included in Our Construction Cleaning

Our construction cleaning program covers every phase of post-construction cleanup:

• Rough clean: removal of large debris, packaging materials, and bulk dust after framing and drywall
• Final clean: detailed dusting of all surfaces, light fixtures, vents, window frames, and ledges
• Floor finishing: sweeping, mopping, scrubbing, and polishing of all floor types (concrete, tile, VCT, hardwood)
• Window and glass cleaning: interior and exterior glass, mirrors, and partitions
• Restroom and kitchen prep: sanitizing all fixtures, counters, and surfaces before occupancy
• Paint and adhesive removal: careful removal of overspray, labels, stickers, and tape residue

We also handle punch-list items and touch-up cleans before final inspections, tenant walkthroughs, or certificate-of-occupancy reviews. Our goal is to hand over a building that's truly ready for business from day one.

We begin with a pre-clean site visit to assess the scope: square footage, number of floors, surface types, timeline, and any special requirements from the general contractor or building owner. From there, we provide a detailed proposal with phased pricing so you know exactly what to expect.
