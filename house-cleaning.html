Affordable House Cleaning Solutions

A clean home is a healthy home. Bel Cleaning provides thorough, dependable residential cleaning services throughout the St. Louis metropolitan area. Whether you need a one-time deep clean before a special event, regular weekly or bi-weekly maintenance, or a move-in/move-out cleaning, our trained staff delivers spotless results every time.

Our residential cleaning crews follow a detailed, room-by-room checklist so nothing gets overlooked. We dust, vacuum, mop, sanitize bathrooms and kitchens, wipe down all surfaces, and pay special attention to high-touch areas like light switches, doorknobs, and cabinet handles. We use eco-friendly, non-toxic products that are safe for children, pets, and allergy-sensitive households.

What's Included in Our House Cleaning Service

Every house cleaning visit covers all living areas, bedrooms, bathrooms, and the kitchen. We vacuum carpets and rugs, mop hard floors, clean and sanitize countertops, scrub sinks and fixtures, clean mirrors and glass, empty trash cans, and make beds on request. Add-on services like interior window cleaning, oven cleaning, refrigerator cleaning, and laundry folding are available at an additional charge.

Why Choose Bel Cleaning for Your Home

Every member of our residential cleaning team is background-checked, insured, and trained on proper cleaning techniques and product safety. We bring all supplies and equipment — you don't need to provide anything. Our flexible scheduling lets you book morning, afternoon, or evening appointments, and we offer satisfaction guarantees on every visit. If something isn't right, we'll come back and re-clean it at no extra cost.
